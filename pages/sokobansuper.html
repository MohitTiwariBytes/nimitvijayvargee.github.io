Sokoban Super

Sprig

A classic tile based sokoban game

The Sprites


The game uses many sprites, despite looking so simple. The player is comprised of 4 sub-sprites for each direction. This allows the player to look more dynamic when moving. The boxes turn into walls when pushed for convinience, because I didn't bother with making the boxes solid when in the hole.

The game will keep 2 extra sprite holders: PlayerDirection and the PreviousDirection. This lets me replace the player with a new sprite facing in the desired direction. As for level design, most levels are easy but feel free to create some of your own! Level 10 was a fun one because it took barely much time to make but a long time to playtest.
